About me

My name is Irena Vieira, I live in the beautiful city of Rotterdam (in the Netherlands) and I'm an aspiring Web Developer.

I have a bachelor's degree in European Studies with a specialisation in Communication Management. However, after graduating I somehow ended up working in the field of Finance. The past 8 years I've held various positions at the biggest consumer bank of the Netherlands, ING Bank N.V. I had a great time working in this field, but it's time for something different. Since I'm fascinated about the increasing influence that technology has on our everyday lives, I figured: why not get into the fascinating world of tech?

Lately, I've been spending most of my time working on learning HTML and CSS and attending tech meetups. When the code on my screen starts to dance in front of my eyes, I like to take a break and meet up with friends, do some yoga or catch up on the latest episode of whatever tv-show is my favourite at that moment.
